DORA

DORA stands for DevOps Research and Assessment.

DORA metrics are key performance indicators used to assess the effectiveness and efficiency of DevOps practices within an organization,

  • Deployment Frequency: Count of successful pipelines.
  • Lead Time For Change: Time taken for a commit to successfully get through staging area.
  • Mean Time To Recover: Recovery time from terminal to success pipeline.
  • Change In Failure Rate: Percentage of failed pipelines.
